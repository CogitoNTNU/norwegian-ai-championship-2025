Reduce the length of hospital stay 1. Reduce the need for surgery ## Enhancing Healthcare Team Outcomes Thermal burns are best managed by an interprofessional team, including a wound care nurse. While the treatment in most patients is supportive, recent evidence indicates that HBO therapy may help wounds heal faster.
