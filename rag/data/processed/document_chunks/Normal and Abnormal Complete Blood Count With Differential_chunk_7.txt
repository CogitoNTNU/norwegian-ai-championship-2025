Nursing, Allied Health, and Interprofessional Team Interventions Any abnormality on the CBC should lead to examination to rule out interfering factors that may contribute to spurious reading, as listed in Table 3. Evaluation of a peripheral blood smear can help exclude factors that may interfere with abnormal CBC readings. Familiarity with the reference range is based on local laboratory parameters and to interpret any abnormalities in the clinical context.
