Educational messages such as the danger of firearms and how they should be safely used and stored can be promoted. ## Enhancing Healthcare Team Outcomes The mortality rate from abdominal gunshot wounds remains high due to the high-velocity, penetrating abdominal trauma, and potential shock from damage to organs and intraperitoneal vascular extravasation.
